The aim of this paper is to assess the impact of dierent interest rate\nscenarios on the banks' interest income. As we do not know the interest rate\nsensitivity of real banks, we construct for each bank a portfolio with a similar\ncomposition of its assets and liabilities, called 'tracking bank'. We evaluate the\neect of 260 historical interest rate shocks on the tracking banks of German\nsavings banks and cooperative banks. It turns out that a sharp decrease in\nthe steepness of the yield curve has the most negative impact on the banks'\ninterest income.\n",
