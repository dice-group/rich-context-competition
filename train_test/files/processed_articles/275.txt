A probabil-\nwas studied (SABE query), focusing on struc-\ntural and functional dimensions of social net-\nworks. Univariate and multiple logistic regres-\nsion models were used to analyze the data. The\nbest conditions in social networks were in the\nlowest income levels and the worst among older\nand unmarried people. For women, unmarried\nmarital status was significantly associated with\nlower schooling. Cohabiting had the strongest\ninfluence on the functional dimension, while\n\"living alone\" significantly decreased the odds\nof social exchange. Multiple logistic regression\nby gender suggested that age, marital status,\nper capita income, and schooling were associ-\nated with social networks among elderly people.\nHowever, these relationships were expressed un-\nevenly between the genders and depending on\nthe socio-demographic characteristics of the dif-\nferent dimensions of the social support network.\nSocial Support; Aged; Social Conditions\nIntrodu\u00e7\u00e3o\nA partir do final dos anos 70 do s\u00e9culo XX, a ca-\ntegoria apoio social tem favorecido o desenvolvi-\nmento de diversos trabalhos de investiga\u00e7\u00e3o que\nbuscam explicar algumas das diferen\u00e7as existen-\ntes na distribui\u00e7\u00e3o de certas enfermidades, tanto\nf\u00edsicas quanto mentais.\nN\u00e3o obstante, at\u00e9 o momento n\u00e3o existe\nconsenso a respeito da defini\u00e7\u00e3o de apoio social,\neste conceito complexo, composto por v\u00e1rias di-\nmens\u00f5es que se associam com a sa\u00fade dos in-\ndiv\u00edduos.\nOs termos mais freq\u00fcentemente encontrados\nna literatura s\u00e3o apoio social e redes sociais, mas\n\u00e9 comum encontrarmos termos como rela\u00e7\u00f5es\nsociais, integra\u00e7\u00e3o social, v\u00ednculos sociais e an-\ncoragem social, sendo empregados indistinta-\nmente 1,2.
